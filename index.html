Welcome to CAVES & PANGAEA Data Portal
The CAVES and PANGAEA specializes in training programmes that equip astronauts with scientific, expeditionary and behavioral skills. The team is primarily focused on two training programmes:
  • CAVES - an expeditionary and human behavioral and performance training course that prepares astronauts for the challenges of spaceflight by having them undertake a real expedition into a natural cave system as a multicultural ISS-representative crew
  • PANGAEA - a field geology and planetary science training course that prepares on astronauts for geological exploration during future missions to the Moon and other planetary bodies using analogue field sites around Europe
Complementary to its training goals, the team is also involved in several areas of research and development that aim to improve the training of astronauts and support future planetary exploration
