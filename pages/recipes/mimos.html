Mimos or Suspiros de Moya

/'Mimes or Moya's sighs'/


Origin

1900s Moya, Gran Canaria, Canary Islands.


Ingredients for 3 servings


  • 1 egg white
  • Splash of lemon juice (1/8 of a lemon)
  • 25 g sugar


Utensils


<
>
Hand mixer
Oven
Piping bag


Steps


  1. Whip ingredients until it develops sharp peaks.
  2. Transfer to piping bag, make the shapes on an oven paper and bake for 1 h @90ÂºC.
