Chicken Makhani

/'Butter chicken'/


Origin


Ingredients for 3 servings


First short marinade

  • 500 g chicken breast in cubes (should be thighs. I think it's okay to use breast though)
  • Salt

Second marinade

  • 0.5 tsp Kashmiri red chili powder (don't substitute it entirely with paprika, the flavor is completely different)
  • 0.3 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 80 g greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp ginger garlic paste (substitute with half ginger half garlic)
  • 0.3 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 0.3 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 12 g olive oil (1 tbsp)

Sauce

  • 2 tbsp ghee (unholy substitute it with half oil half butter, 24 g)
  • Cinnamon stick (1)
  • Green cardamoms (3)
  • Cloves (3)
  • 8 tomatoes (or 260 g passata. Read culinary tip #2)
  • 2 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • Green chili (1, deseeded, slit or chopped)
  • Raw cashews (28)
  • 1 tsp Kashmiri red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 0.3 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder

Finishing

  • 90 g heavy cream
  • 0.5 tbsp Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves, crushed)
  • 1 tbsp chopped coriander leaves


Utensils


<
>
Kitchen pot
Oven


Steps


First short marinade

  1. Prepare the chicken in medium cubes, removing bones and extra fat.
  2. Add some salt to it, leave aside.

Second marinade

  1. Prepare and mix the spices.
  2. Spread it on the chicken and leave it covered for at least 8 h.

Grilling the chicken

You can either do this in a pan or in the oven. I use the oven. After pre-heating, set at 220ºC (convection) for 21'.

Prepare the next spice mix and leave the cashews on some boiling water

Making the sauce

  1. On the same pot, open a spot on the center and add the ingredients listed, previously mixed, except the tomato and cashews.
  2. After 1-2', before the spices burn, add some water with the passata / blended tomatoes / tomatoes boiled and crushed separately.
  3. Blend the cashew liquid finely, pour it in.

Finishing

  1. After left out for some time, you should be able to see traces of fat on top. Add heavy cream and reduce for 2-3'.
  2. Throw in the fine leaves. Doing so before is also possible but they will turn black.

CULINARY TIP

Some people add onions. But the whole point of butter chicken is having a silky smooth sauce. Onions will mess with it.

CULINARY TIP

To counteract tomato acidity, instead of adding sugar or onions, you can slowly add to the sauce sodium bicarbonate. Mix it and taste it after every time.

CULINARY TIP

If you are not used to hot spicy food, reduce the amount of Kashmiri chili powder.
